To Professor Sue Sampson:

I talked on Discord about the Intro to Cyber Threats assignment to decrypt a message using 4 different ciphers.  What I didn't say on Discord was the story behind the message we decrypted.  

The message was "Super Mario Professor."  So this story comes from: this particular professor recorded a short video, about 15-20 minutes long, each week in which he would answer questions anyone had from the previous week, touch on problem areas in the assignments, and cover the basics of what we were covering that week. On a shelf behind his desk, there was a lamp in the shape of the Super Star from Super Mario Bros.  

My son, Elliott, who was 5 at the time, came up to me while I was watching one of these to ask what I was watching.  I told him I was doing my schoolwork, and he IMMEDIATELY spotted the Super Star and dubbed him the "Super Mario Professor.”  Now, this was in the summer term of 2024 (so nearly two years ago), but every time I am doing homework, he still asks, "Is that for the Super Mario Professor?"

I will say that class nearly made me want to change my major to Cybersecurity...  But I still enjoy programming more.  :)
